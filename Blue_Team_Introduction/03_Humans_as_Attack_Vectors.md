# Humans as Attack Vectors

**Goal / Objective:**  
Humans are the weakest element in cybersecurity.
Attackers target people because of the access they can provide—to websites, mailboxes, or internal databases.

**Key Terms / Concepts:**  
	•	Social Engineering: Manipulating people to gain unauthorized access
	•	Phishing: Fraudulent emails or messages designed to trick users
	•	Insider Threat: Employees or contractors who intentionally or accidentally compromise security
	•	Deepfakes: AI-generated fake audio or video used for impersonation
	•	Impersonation: Pretending to be a trusted person to gain access
	•   Mitigation: Prevent or reduce the chance and impact of human-targeted attacks using tools like anti-phishing solutions, security awareness training, and multi-factor authentication.


**Tips / Notes:**  
Humans are the weakest link in cybersecurity
Email phishing is the most common form of social engineering
