# Junior Security Analyst Intro

**Goal / Objective:**  
Understand the role of a Junior SOC Analyst, including basic duties, workflows, and how to respond to security alerts.

**Key Terms / Concepts:**  
- **SOC:** Security Operations Center  
- **Alert:** Notification about potential security incident  
- **Triage:** Prioritizing alerts based on severity  
- **SOC Team Roles:** Senior Analyst, SOC Engineer, SOC Manager, Incident Responder

**Practical Steps / Exercises:**  
- **Duties of a Junior SOC Analyst:**  
  1. Detect and prevent a data stealer infection on a coworker's laptop  
  2. Analyze and stop a phishing campaign targeting the finance team  
  3. Participate in larger incidents, such as full-scale ransomware attacks  
  4. Collaborate with teammates to build detection rules and automations  
  5. Understand company operations to enhance security measures
